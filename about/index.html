About Us

Our number one goal is to exceed the customer’s expectations. We cater to our customers by giving them a customized plan that meets their landscaping and grounds maintenance needs.

We are a full-service landscape and lawn maintenance contractor with experience in commercial and residential lawn services. Established in 2003, Showcase is managed by an owner that has over 20 years of experience in commercial/residential services.

Our staff consists of several qualified employees that will complete the job successfully and professionally. We also provide our employees a safe environment to work in and we adhere to all OSHA’s regulations. All equipment that is used on the job sites is either in new condition or is properly maintained. Our company can provide services for both small scale or large scale jobs as well as providing services in town or out of town.

Showcase is licensed, fully insured, and provides Workman’s Compensation coverage for all employees.
