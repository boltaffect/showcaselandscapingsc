Lawn Maintenance Services

The landscaping of your property complements your lifestyle and works as an accent to the architecture of your home or business. At Showcase, our Lawn Maintenance Program is customized according to the specific needs of each client. Because we are on-site for 52 weeks per year, you will be able to enjoy the greater beauty and detail in your landscape throughout the year.

Showcase believes in quality and professional service. Our staff will treat your property in the most professional way. Under the regular care of our professional horticultural staff, your gardens and landscape will look immaculate year around.

  • Aeration & Seeding
  • Pruning
  • Fertilization
  • Mulching
  • Perennials & Ornamental Grass
  • Leaf Removal

  • Landscape Lighting
  • Inspections
  • Irrigation Inspections
